Plan of Our Relief Team

We collect emergency funds to help the victims at flood-affected areas with essential remedy and reliefs. We also aim to aid in these impacted areas' post-flood rehabilitation functions.

Reaching affected areas

We are alarmed by the grim news coming from many areas, where there are increasing pleas for emergency relief amidst inadequate funding and sluggish rescue operations. We reach to those areas as soon as we can.

Food distribution

Through our flood relief drive, we provid ration packages filled with dry food items, ready-to-eat food packets, dry snacks, packs of biscuits, muri, milk powder, and drinking water to flood-affected families.

Providing first aid kits

During flood, various diseases gets spread via water and animals. We provide medicine, saline and first aid kits for remedy of these. Hygiene products, such as soaps and sanitary napkins are also included.
